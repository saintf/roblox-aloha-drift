# Aloha Drift — Game Overview

## Elevator Pitch
Aloha Drift is a tropical-futuristic faction game on Roblox where rival teams race hover vehicles across floating islands, raid each other's bases, haul glowing crystals, and compete for sky territory — all without a single death screen. Fall off an island? You splash into the warm ocean below, spot a mermaid, and teleport cheerfully home.

## Working Title
**Aloha Drift**

## Platform
Roblox (Lua/Luau). Server-authoritative architecture throughout. Designed for mobile and PC.

## Target Audience
All ages, safe for under-13. Comparable tone and complexity to Emergency Hamburg. No blood, no death animations — consequences are expressed through displacement and loot loss.

## Aesthetic
Sun-soaked tropical-futuristic. Bioluminescent palm trees, turquoise oceans, pastel hover-tech towns, rolling green hills, bold cartoon-real art style with thick outlines and saturated colours. Think Fortnite's accessibility and charm with the warmth of a Pacific island summer. Every asset should feel like it belongs on a permanent vacation that also happens to have jetpacks.

> **Meshy prompt seed for all assets:**
> "Tropical futuristic floating island, bioluminescent palm trees, pastel coral and teal hover-tech buildings, bold cartoon-real style, thick outlines, saturated warm colours, Pacific island summer, Fortnite-adjacent proportions, chunky sci-fi gadgets"

## Core Fantasy
- Zoom across open sky on a hover-bike to raid an enemy base
- Crack a safe while a defender chases you with a wind blaster
- Pilot a lumbering crystal barge home while rivals try to tether and hijack you
- Coordinate with your faction to research team perks and fortify your island
- Fall off an edge, spot a mermaid, and immediately get back in the action

## Key Differentiators from Emergency Hamburg
- Faction vs faction (team competition vs individual roles in a shared city)
- Custom hover vehicle physics (not standard Roblox vehicles)
- Displacement-not-death consequence system
- Floating island cluster map with altitude layers
- Crystal cargo hauling as a core PvP loop

## Monetisation Philosophy
Gamepasses accelerate progression — they never gate fun. Free players can reach maximum tier in every role through playtime alone. The gap between best free vehicle and best paid vehicle is ≤15% in raw stats. Cosmetics are the primary revenue driver.
